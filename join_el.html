 The Indian Navy as a Career

    Eligibility Conditions for Navy are:

Info Details
Age Between 19-1/2 to 25 years (candidates to be born between 02 January 1984 to 01 July 1989 both dates inclusive)
Educational Qualifications Science Graduate with minimum 60% marks in Physics, Mathematics or Electronics OR Post Graduate in Science with minimum 55% marks in Physics, Mathematics or Electronics. (Those who have not complete thier Degree, need not apply).
Physical Standards Hight and Wieght : Minimum height Male - 157 cms, Female - 152 cms with correlated weight. Eye Sight - The minimum acceptable standard for distant vision 6/6, 6/9 correctable to 6/6, 6/6 with glasses and should not be colour/night blind.
